# Rayact support policy

Rayact supports the latest two stable minor lines or twelve months from release, whichever is longer. Deprecations receive at least one stable minor and 90 days of notice before removal.

Tier-1 macOS support is Apple Silicon (arm64) on macOS 13 or newer. Intel Macs and Rosetta are outside the supported product matrix. This does not remove Android x86_64 emulator or iOS simulator coverage.

Security reports are acknowledged within two business days, triaged within five business days, and critical fixes target release within seven days. General support is provided through GitHub issues and discussions for supported releases and documented Tier-1 configurations.
